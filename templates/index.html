About

Circle K International (CKI) is a global student-led organization dedicated to community service, leadership development, and fostering fellowship among college students. With a focus on making a positive impact, CKI empowers members to engage in meaningful volunteer activities that address local and global challenges. Through service projects, fundraising efforts, and leadership training, CKI members develop essential skills while building strong connections with fellow students. By participating in CKI, individuals not only contribute to their communities but also grow personally and professionally, creating a network of like-minded peers committed to making a difference.
